You Can’t Work While
You’re Recharging...

But your phone can!

Introducing SynapseGL, the first platform to monetize the world’s fastest growing untapped computing resource.

It has become conventional wisdom that your phone packs more raw computational power than anything in NASA’s possession when they landed on the moon. A lesser known fact is that the performance of your phone’s CPU (Central Processing Unit) is dwarfed by the computational power of its GPU (Graphics Processing Unit). The former is built and optimized for energy efficiency, since it is always on. The latter is designed to dazzle us when processing photos and video or while playing games on our handhelds.
