There are currently 2.8 billion mobile devices worldwide, where the average user spends 8 hours of it charging when they are sleeping.
With over 8.5 billion transistors in the new Apple A14 processor with dedicated GPU chipsets, the power within them are not to be looked down upon.

Recently it was announced that Apple will be switching their Macbook lineup with the ARM based architecture, the main design behind current smartphone technology.

Currently, the GPUs within our smartphones are turned off for efficency purposes and is only activated when video or picture playback is required.

SynapseGL looks to leverage your untapped phone power and package it as a service to demanding industries worldwide. We provide you the market that offers pricing based on amount of computational power you share within our network.
We look to be a real time, demand driven platform that allocates phone computation efficently, fairly and evenly thoroughout processes worldwide.
Some initial use cases with aggregated phone computation consist of:

  • CGI
  • VFX
  • Scientific Computing
  • Machine Learning
